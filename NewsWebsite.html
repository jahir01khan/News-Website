Pakistan Slams US: Terrorists Getting Hold of Uncle Sam's Leftover Arms from Afghanistan

Pakistan's caretaker PM Anwaar-Ul-Haq Kakar believes that US weapons, which are easily available in the black market, are being used for terrorism in Pakistan, a media report said. Kakar dismissed the significance of the United States acknowledging or denying the use of its weapons in Pakistan, pointing to tangible proof that these weapons are entering the illicit markets and are being actively utilized. He affirmed that Pakistan's position is based on solid evidence, not unfounded speculations.
